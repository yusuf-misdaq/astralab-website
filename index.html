About Astralab

Our Story

Astralab is an alchemical community third-space in the heart of Los Angeles, we cater to all but specialize in diaspora wisdom, creativity, health, spirituality.

Our Mission

Astralab is a cultural incubator dedicated to ancestral intelligence and sacred innovation. Rooted in the historic Granada Buildings — a timeless crossroads echoing the spirit of the old Silk Road — we host a vibrant array of cultural exchange workshops, concerts, art exhibitions, salons, and bazaars. We serve as a community hub and sanctuary, where artists, healers, visionaries, and seekers gather to explore and co-create within the framework of the sacred economy. Astralab is a living invitation to remember, reconnect, and reimagine what becomes possible when ancestral wisdom meets modern creativity.

The Space

Our historic / epic space is best described as Central Asia meets minimalism, we have beautiful lighting solutions, powerful audio system, and a truly distinctive atmosphere that adapts and serves endless visions.

The Founders

Astralab was founded by the two visionaries, Yusuf Misdaq & Christina Lila Wilson.
